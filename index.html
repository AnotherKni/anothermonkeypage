Scene Switcher And Finder

Scene Switcher And Finder is a tool created for Unity developers looking to simplify scene management in their projects. With it, you can quickly switch between different scenes in your project, mark scenes as favorites for easy access, and track a history of recent scenes. The interface is user-friendly and customizable to suit your needs.
